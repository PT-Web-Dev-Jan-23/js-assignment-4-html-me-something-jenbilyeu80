On January 28, 2022, Dakotah Kelly performed her first solo at
Believe Talent dance Competition. Throughout the day, Dakotah struggles with anxiety.
Dance allows her to escape her thoughts and fears. She once told me she gets panic attacks before going on stage.
As soon as the music starts, her mind and body calms down, and
memory muscle takes over. She can smile again.
